Harry and the Spell Power

Harry Potter is learning different spells. He has a list of spells with their power values. He wants to know which spell is the most powerful and which the least powerful. Print: "Most powerful: ...-Least powerful: ..."


Sample Input:
expetum-98 patronus-23 Axio-89 lithigum-33 madriketom-76

Sample Output:
Most powerful: Spell(name=expetum, power=98)-Least powerful: Spell(name=patronus, power=23)



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 8 seconds
