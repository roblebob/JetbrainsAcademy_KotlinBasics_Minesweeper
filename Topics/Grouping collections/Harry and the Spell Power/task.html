Harry and the Spell Power

Harry Potter is learning different spells. He has a list of spells with their power values. He wants to group them by their power.


Sample Input:
expetum-50 patronus-20 axio-50 lithigum-20 madriketom-70

Sample Output:
{50=[Spell(name=expetum, power=50), Spell(name=axio, power=50)], 20=[Spell(name=patronus, power=20), Spell(name=lithigum, power=20)], 70=[Spell(name=madriketom, power=70)]}



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 8 seconds
